Contact Singapore Airlines Customer Service

Reach out to a live representative anytime via phone, chat, email, or social media.

📞 24/7 Customer Service Hotline

For immediate assistance, call Singapore Airlines' main customer service line:

  • Phone: +1 (888) 829-1117
  • TTY/TDD (Hearing Impaired): +1 (833) 727-0118

Have your booking reference, SkyMiles number, and travel details ready for faster service.

💬 Live Chat Support

Prefer not to wait on hold? Use Singapore Airlines' live chat feature:

  • Website: Start Live Chat
  • Mobile App: Open the app, navigate to the “Help” or “Contact Us” section, and start a chat session.

Best for quick questions, minor booking adjustments, and when you can’t make a call.

📧 Email Support

For non-urgent concerns or when you need to send documents (like refund requests or medical certificates), email is ideal:

  • General Inquiries: customerqueries@singaporeair.com.sg
  • Complaints or Claims: onlineform@singaporeair.com.sg

Response time is usually within a few business days.

📱 Mobile App Support

The Fly Singapore Airlines app isn’t just for checking in. You can manage bookings, chat with support, and even request callbacks:

  • Download the app: Available on iOS and Android
  • Access Help: Log in, and access the “Help” section for support options.

📱 WhatsApp Support (If Available)

Some regions offer WhatsApp support for Singapore Airlines. Check the official website for the latest contact details.

Best for quick queries when you have limited phone access.

🌐 Social Media Support

Singapore Airlines is active on platforms like Twitter and Facebook. Sending a direct message often gets a quick response, especially for public complaints or quick clarifications:

  • Twitter: @SingaporeAir
  • Facebook Messenger: Singapore Airlines

Best for non-urgent issues, sharing feedback, or getting updates on widespread disruptions.

✈️ Airport Customer Service Desk

If you’re already at the airport and need immediate assistance—like rebooking after a cancellation—visit the Singapore Airlines service desk:

  • Location: At all major airports, near check-in or boarding gates.
  • Best for: Last-minute changes, baggage issues, or special travel needs.

🌐 Official Website for FAQs and Self-Service

Many issues can be resolved without contacting an agent. The Singapore Airlines website offers comprehensive FAQs, booking management tools, and travel advisories:

  • Website: Contact Us

For the most accurate and up-to-date contact information, always refer to the official Singapore Airlines Contact Us page.
